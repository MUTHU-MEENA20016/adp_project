WHAT IS POETRY GENRE?

Poetry is a genre of literature that defies conventional boundaries, encompassing a myriad of styles, forms, and thematic explorations. Poetry transcends the limitations of straightforward prose, weaving together words, rhythm, and imagery to create a tapestry of emotions, experiences, and perspectives.

QUALITIES IN POETRY :

  • RYTHM AND METER
  • IMAGERY AND SYMBOLISM
  • SOUND DEVICES
  • CONCISION AMD PRECISION
